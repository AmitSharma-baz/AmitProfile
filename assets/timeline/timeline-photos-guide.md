## Technical Specifications

### Image Requirements:
- **Format:** JPG (preferred) or PNG
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1200x800px (landscape) or 800x1200px (portrait)
- **Quality:** High resolution, well-lit, clear focus
- **File Size:** Optimized for web (under 500KB each)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 3:2 or 4:3 preferred for consistency

### Photo Quality Guidelines:
1. **Professional Quality:** Clear, well-composed photographs
2. **Good Lighting:** Avoid dark or poorly lit images
3. **Clear Subjects:** Amit Sharma should be clearly visible and identifiable
4. **Context Appropriate:** Photos should match the described activities
5. **Authentic:** Real photos from actual events (not stock photos)

### Naming Convention:
- Use the exact filenames specified (e.g., `2018-1.jpg`, `2019-2.jpg`)
- Maintain consistent naming across all years
- Use lowercase extensions (.jpg, .png)

## Photo Content Guidelines

### What to Include:
- Amit Sharma prominently featured in photos
- Relevant BJP branding/logos where appropriate
- Community members and beneficiaries
- Event banners and signage
- Action shots showing actual work/activities
- Group photos with party members/officials
- Award/recognition ceremonies
- Community service activities

### What to Avoid:
- Blurry or low-quality images
- Photos where Amit Sharma is not clearly visible
- Inappropriate or unprofessional content
- Copyrighted images without permission
- Photos that don't match the timeline context

## Implementation Notes

### Fallback System:
- If actual photos are not available, placeholder images will be shown
- Placeholder text: "Photo Coming Soon"
- Users will see a notification when photos are not yet available

### Photo Modal Features:
- **Carousel View:** Navigate through multiple photos per year
- **Thumbnails:** Quick navigation between photos
- **Captions:** Each photo has title and description
- **Keyboard Navigation:** Arrow keys for navigation
- **Responsive Design:** Works on all devices

### SEO and Accessibility:
- All images should have descriptive alt text
- Captions provide context for screen readers
- Images are optimized for fast loading
- Responsive images for different screen sizes
