My Political Journey

From grassroots activism to BJP Yuva Mahamantri - A journey of service and dedication

2018

Political Awakening

Started my political journey by joining BJP as a volunteer. Participated in local community development programs and began understanding grassroots politics in Ward 30, Surat.

First BJP Membership Community Volunteer Ward 30 Focus
2019

Youth Leadership Role

Appointed as Youth Coordinator for Ward 30. Organized first blood donation camp and started regular community outreach programs. Gained recognition for youth mobilization efforts.

Youth Coordinator First Blood Camp Community Outreach
2020

COVID-19 Service

During the pandemic, led relief efforts in Ward 30. Distributed food packets, organized medical assistance, and coordinated with local authorities for community safety measures.

Pandemic Relief Food Distribution Medical Assistance
Full COVID Service
2021

Election Campaign Leadership

Successfully managed BJP's local election campaign in Ward 30. Organized rallies, voter awareness programs, and door-to-door campaigns. Achieved significant voter turnout increase.

Campaign Manager Rally Organization Voter Awareness
2022

Recognition & Awards

Received recognition from BJP Gujarat for outstanding youth leadership and community service. Expanded blood donation program to cover multiple areas in Surat Mahanagar.

BJP Recognition Youth Leadership Program Expansion
2023

Yuva Mahamantri Appointment

Appointed as BJP Yuva Mahamantri for Ward 30, Surat Mahanagar. Launched comprehensive youth development programs and strengthened party presence in the constituency.

Yuva Mahamantri Youth Development Party Leadership
2024

Expanding Horizons

Launched digital initiatives, established regular health camps, and created employment opportunities for youth. Currently working on infrastructure development projects for Ward 30.

Digital Initiatives Health Camps Infrastructure Focus
