My Political Journey

As a dedicated member of the Bharatiya Janata Party and serving as Yuva Mahamantri for Ward 30 in Kansad-Sachin-Unn-Aabhva, I am committed to bringing positive change to our community through youth-driven initiatives and grassroots leadership.

My journey with BJP began with a vision to serve the people and contribute to the development of Surat. Through various social initiatives, community programs, and active political participation, I strive to be the voice of the youth and a bridge between the community and the government.
