COVID Relief Services

Comprehensive support during the pandemic

Food Distribution

Organized daily food packet distribution for families affected by lockdown, ensuring no one went hungry during the crisis.

  • Daily meal distribution
  • Grocery kits for families
  • Special nutrition for elderly
  • Emergency food supplies

Medical Assistance

Coordinated medical support, oxygen supply, and hospital bed arrangements for COVID patients and their families.

  • Oxygen cylinder supply
  • Hospital bed coordination
  • Medicine distribution
  • Ambulance services

Safety Measures

Implemented safety protocols, distributed masks and sanitizers, and conducted awareness campaigns in the community.

  • Mask distribution drives
  • Sanitizer supply
  • Awareness campaigns
  • Safety protocol education

24/7 Helpline

Established round-the-clock helpline for emergency assistance, information, and coordination of relief services.

  • Emergency contact service
  • Information helpdesk
  • Coordination support
  • Crisis counseling

Essential Services

Ensured delivery of essential services including groceries, medicines, and other necessities to doorsteps.

  • Doorstep delivery
  • Essential goods supply
  • Pharmacy coordination
  • Emergency supplies

Community Support

Mobilized volunteers and community resources to provide comprehensive support to affected families and individuals.

  • Volunteer coordination
  • Community mobilization
  • Resource management
  • Support networks
